Basic Warmup

Elements distilled over the years from various types of gentle exercise.

Lower Body
  • Ankle Circle
  • Hip Flex, Extend
  • Hip ABduct, Adduct
  • Hip Rotate (Open)
  • 1/2 Squat
  • Kick, Butt Kick
  • Straight Leg Raise, Mule Kick
  • Side Kick, Adduct
  • Hip Rotate (Closed)
  • Lateral 3/4 Squat
  • Calf Raise, Shin Raise
  • Gentle Knee Circles
Upper Body
  • Shrug, Rear Shrug
  • Shoulder Raise Out, Front, Back
  • Reach Overhead
  • Curl (Wrist Rotation)
  • Wrist Pronate, Supinate
  • Wrist Flex, Extend, Ulnar, Radial
  • Hands, Fingers
  • Shoulder Rotators Up, Down, Out
  • Neck Rotate, Lateral, Flex, Extend
  • Progressive Arm Rotation
Core

Easy does it with back exercises!

  • Hip Circle
  • Back Lateral Flex
  • Deadlift
  • Spine Rotation Upper, lower
  • 45 Degree Cat, Camel
  • Cat, Camel
  • Floppy Pushup, Child Pose
Optional Harder Warm Up
  • DB Shoulder Shrug
  • DB Bent Low Row
  • 1/2 Squat
  • Mil Press
  • Pushup
  • Lunge
  • Plank, Side Plank
  • Stretch Squat (no load)
